# Soccer polyhedron

A soccer polyhedron, [truncated icosahedron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Truncated_icosahedron),  is one of the most familiar [spherical polyhedron](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spherical_polyhedron).
